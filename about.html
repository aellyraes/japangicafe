Our Story

Korean Boutique Cafe, established in 2018, is called "Japangi Cafe 자판기 카페," which means a vending machine in Korean. The café’s classy entrance and dark red interior make it a standout, offering a unique atmosphere that welcomes guests to experience a blend of modern style and traditional Korean warmth.

All the dishes served at Japangi Cafe are authentic, with ingredients sourced directly from South Korea. This commitment to quality ensures a true Korean dining experience. Our menu features a variety of traditional Korean dishes, including Korean dosirak, ramyeon, gimbap, egg pockets, and more, bringing the flavors of Korea to your table.

In addition to our delicious dishes, we offer a wide range of beverages, including milk tea, iced tea, healthy fruit tea, and healthy tea bags, among others. Whether you're craving a refreshing drink or a comforting meal, Japangi Cafe provides a cozy and welcoming atmosphere for you to enjoy the authentic taste of Korean cuisine in every bite and sip.
